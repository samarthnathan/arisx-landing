The problem

Collections wears customers down instead of resolving them.

  • 01

    Infinite calls, infinite haggling

    Endless calls and messages turn even a high-intent customer into a low-intent one.

  • 02

    No path to clear progressively

    Customers can't settle their loans step by step — it's lump-sum or nothing.

Our belief

Pull, not push, structurally improves collections efficiency.

Five different agents call the same customer whenever they happen to connect, each starting the conversation from zero, with zero context carried over — leaving the chance of resolution down to luck, not design.

One customer, four uncoordinated pulls
Pulled four different ways, resolved by none.

When every agent starts from zero and pulls toward their own script, the customer isn't being helped toward a plan — they're being stretched between five conversations that don't know about each other. Coordination isn't a nicety here; it's what turns that same pull into a resolution.

Our solution

One platform, three parts.

01 · For lenders

Back-office automation

For banks, NBFCs and ARCs — built into allocation, settlement approval, and document management, so every conversation reconciles against your systems.

02 · For customers

Self-serve portal

A single place for customers to view their offer, agree a progressive plan, and settle — without another call or branch visit.

03 · For adoption

Educational campaigns

Outreach that explains the plan and builds trust before the ask — so the first conversation moves toward resolution, not haggling.
